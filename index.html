Our Story

Welcome

We first opened the doors to Café Vienna in 2005. It all began with just one breakfast sandwich and one Austrian baker behind the counter selling our store designed European-style breads.

Over the years, the bakery grew into a full-service cafe offering breakfast, lunch, dinner, and catering. Of course, we also continue our authentic German-Austrian pastries that are made fresh in store daily, such as Sacher Torte and Bienenstich. Our famous Altwiener Apfelstrudel, for instance, was featured on “Chicago’s Best” show in 2012.

The line of hungry Chicagoans at the door on weekends is proof that Cafe Vienna has become a favorite dining spot for it's Lincoln Park neighborhood. Through the Cafe's participation in events like Taste of Chicago, May Fest, Christmas market and many other Chicago-based festivals, word about the food has spread and the Cafe's popularity continues to increase.

Due to our much appreciated customer loyalty, Cafe Vienna is truly a local Lincoln Park and Chicago success story and we're excited to see what the future brings.

We are always open online to take your order. You can pick it up anytime when the store is open. Thanks for being a passionate Café Vienna customer!
